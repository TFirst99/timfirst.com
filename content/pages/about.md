My name is Tim First, and I am a 23 year old research assistant at the University of Chicago. This site contains my research and some of my writings.

# Undergraduate
<hr>
I grew up outside of Boston, Massachusetts, and did my undergraduate studies at Connecticut College. I graduated with degrees in economics and philosophy, and split my time between the varsity squash team and an on-campus coffee shop that I ran my senior year.

# Edinburgh
<hr>
During my junior year I studied abroad at the University of Edinburgh. While in Scotland, I took courses in economics and math and absolutely fell in love with the city. To this day Edinburgh is probably my favorite city in the world.
